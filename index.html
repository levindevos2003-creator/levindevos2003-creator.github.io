Bachelor's thesis · KU Leuven · 2025

Confocal Imaging: Speed vs. Resolution

I compared the TriScan line-scanning microscope with the Leica TCS SP8, the point-scanning confocal most labs still reach for. Using TetraSpeck beads and fixed HeLa cells, I measured resolution and speed at 488, 561, and 638 nm. The short version: the TriScan ran up to ten times faster, though you pay for it in Z-axis sharpness. Handy to know before you trust the prettier picture.
